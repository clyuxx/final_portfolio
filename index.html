ABOUT ME

I grew up loving beautiful things, flowers and plants on the street, children playing, couples kissing, old people walking. I feel that my lens can always capture these beautiful moments. This also led me to the field of photography. Slowly I also began to accumulate some ability about video editing, and uiux design. As a photographer and video editor, I love to speak through the camera and I will express every moving story through my lens. For me, photos or videos, are all very attractive. I can share their stories with the viewers through my lens. I want to use my lens to make my characters shine.
